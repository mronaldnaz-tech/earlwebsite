🌍 7 Continents of the World

Learn first, then play the quiz 😊

🌏 Asia

Asia is the largest continent. It has many countries, foods, and cultures.

🌍 Africa

Africa is known for wildlife like lions, elephants, and wide deserts.

🌍 Europe

Europe has famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and rich history.

🌎 North America

North America has big cities, mountains, and natural wonders.

🌎 South America

South America is home to the Amazon rainforest and many animals.

🌏 Australia

Australia is known for kangaroos, beaches, and unique wildlife.

❄️ Antarctica

Antarctica is the coldest continent. Penguins live here.
